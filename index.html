Nobel Prize Project

Nobel Prize is a prestigious prize awarded separately in the fields of Physics, Chemistry, Physiology or Medicine, Literature, Peace, and Economics, to those who, during the preceding year, have conferred the greatest benefit to humankind. It was established in 1895 by Swedish inventor Alfred Nobel and first awarded in 1901.
